# SEO Audit Report - İsveç'te Şirket Kurma

**Target Keyword**: İsveç'te Şirket Kurma
**Site**: isvecenasilgelinir.com

## 1. Executive Summary
The site currently lacks a dedicated authority page for business establishment in Sweden. While the general migration SEO is solid, the "Business" niche is under-served.

## 2. Technical Audit Findings
- **Title Tag**: The current title "İsveç'e Nasıl Gelinir? | Vize, Oturum ve Çalışma Rehberi 2026" is too broad.
- **H1 Tag**: "İsveç Hayalinizi Gerçeğe Dönüştürün" is generic.
- **Schema Gaps**: 
    - Missing `FAQPage` schema for common business questions.
    - Missing `ProfessionalService` or `Organization` detailed schema.
- **Mobile Usability**: Excellent.
- **Page Speed**: Excellent.

## 3. Competitive Landscape
- **Main Competitor**: Wise (Turkey/Global).
- **Competitor Strength**: High domain authority, broad guides.
- **Competitor Weakness**: Content is translated and generic. Lacks specific local insights on Swedish banking (SEB, Swedbank), Bolagsverket nuances, and Turkish-Swedish double taxation agreements.

## 4. Recommendations
- Create a Pillar Page: `blog/isvecte-sirket-kurma.html`.
- Targeting H2s: "İsveç'te Anonim Şirket (AB) Kurma Şartları", "Enskild Firma vs. AB", "İsveç'te Şirket Kurma Maliyeti".
- Implement FAQ Schema.
